About Galkot Harichaur Haven

Galkot Harichaur Haven is a peaceful countryside retreat located in the heart of Baglung district, surrounded by lush hills and scenic beauty. Our resort offers a blend of tradition and comfort, perfect for nature lovers, travelers, and families looking to relax and explore.

Our Story

Started in 2024 by a group of passionate locals, our mission has always been to preserve the natural environment while offering heartfelt hospitality. What began as a small guesthouse is now a full-fledged resort with comfortable rooms, delicious local food, and personalized services.

What Makes Us Special

  • Traditional architecture with modern amenities
  • Organic food from nearby farms
  • Eco-friendly and family-run environment
  • Close to hiking trails and cultural sites
