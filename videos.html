Welcome to Galkot Harichaur Haven

Your home away from home in the heart of nature.

Video Gallery

Cultural Shows on Request

“Celebrate Heritage with Every Beat” Enjoy vibrant cultural performances with traditional music and dance. Available on advance request, these shows offer a glimpse into the region’s rich heritage—perfect for guests seeking an authentic local experience.

Events & Celebrations

“Your Special Moments, Our Perfect Venue” From birthdays and weddings to religious gatherings, we offer space, decoration, and local catering to make your celebrations memorable and stress-free in a naturally beautiful countryside setting.

College Students Welcome

“Create College Memories That Last” Perfect for group outings or winter camps. Students enjoy open-sky meals, campfires, music, and traditional experiences in a safe, enjoyable atmosphere.

Open-Sky Lunch, Dinner & Picnics

“Enjoy food in a group” Enjoy lunch or dinner in a rustic setup under the open sky with fresh air, firewood cooking, and starry night ambience.

Traditional Fishing Experience in the River

“Catch the Thrill, Not Just the Fish" Join local experts and learn the art of traditional fishing—no rods, no nets, just skill and hands. A rare, hands-on cultural adventure.

Adventure Treks

“Where Every Journey Begins." Ideal for trekking and adventure groups, our location serves as a peaceful base camp. Rest well, eat local, and start your exploration into the surrounding hills, trails, and jungles.
